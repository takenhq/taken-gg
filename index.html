What is taken.gg?

taken.gg is a free username availability checker that lets you instantly search whether a specific username is available across multiple platforms including TikTok, X (formerly Twitter), Instagram, Roblox, and Discord.

What Data We Collect

  • Username queries — the usernames you type into the search field.
  • Selected platforms — which platforms you choose to check.
  • Basic server logs — IP address and user agent, collected automatically for security and rate-limiting purposes.
  • Optional contact info — if you sign up for future "Watch" alerts (coming soon), we may collect your email address or webhook URL.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We never ask for or store passwords to any third-party platform.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide results — to query platforms and return availability status.
  • Prevent abuse — rate limiting and bot protection to keep the service fair for everyone.
  • Improve reliability — aggregated, anonymized analytics to monitor uptime and performance.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use only essential cookies and local storage to remember your preferences such as platform selection and theme. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

taken.gg may be hosted via services such as Cloudflare Pages or similar infrastructure providers. If analytics are used, we prioritize minimal, privacy-friendly solutions. No personally identifiable data is shared with analytics providers.

Data Retention

Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days and are then automatically deleted, unless longer retention is required for security investigations or legal obligations.
